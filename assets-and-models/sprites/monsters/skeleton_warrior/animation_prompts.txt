# Skeleton Warrior - Animation Prompts (9 Frames)

Base prompt: Dungeons and Dragons dark fantasy anime illustration, undead skeleton warrior character, animated skeleton with glowing blue eye sockets, wearing rusted chainmail armor and tattered dark cloak, wielding a longsword and round wooden shield. Viewed from top-down angle (80-90 degrees), standing on circular stone base, 2D painted illustration style, solid dark grey background NO CHECKERED PATTERN.

---

## Frame 1 - Idle Rest
Skeleton at neutral stance, sword at side, shield held defensively, subtle blue glow from eyes, bones at rest position.

## Frame 2 - Begin Movement
Skeleton begins to shift weight, head turns 10 degrees left, shield arm lifts slightly, sword arm tenses, eye glow intensifies.

## Frame 3 - Mid Turn
Head turned 20 degrees left, torso twisting, shield raised 15 degrees, sword pulled back slightly, bones creaking motion.

## Frame 4 - Peak Turn
Maximum turn - head 30 degrees left, shield fully raised protecting left side, sword arm extended right for balance, eye sockets blazing blue.

## Frame 5 - Hold Position
Maintaining turned position, shield high, sword ready, scanning left side, maximum alertness.

## Frame 6 - Begin Return
Starting to turn back, head rotating toward center, shield lowering, sword arm relaxing, eye glow dimming.

## Frame 7 - Mid Return
Head at 15 degrees from center, shield halfway down, sword returning to side, bones settling.

## Frame 8 - Almost Rest
Nearly back to neutral, head 5 degrees from forward, shield almost at rest, sword at side, minimal eye glow.

## Frame 9 - Complete Loop
Identical to Frame 1, ready to loop seamlessly, all bones at starting positions.

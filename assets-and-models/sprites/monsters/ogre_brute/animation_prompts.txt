# Ogre Brute - Animation Prompts (9 Frames)

Base prompt: Dungeons and Dragons dark fantasy anime illustration, ogre brute character, massive muscular humanoid with grey-green skin and brutish ugly face with tusks, wearing crude leather straps and fur loincloth, wielding an enormous wooden club with metal spikes. Viewed from top-down angle (80-90 degrees), standing on circular cracked earth base, 2D painted illustration style, solid dark grey background NO CHECKERED PATTERN.

---

## Frame 1 - Idle Rest
Ogre standing with club resting on shoulder, relaxed menacing stance, breathing normally.

## Frame 2 - Shift Weight
Weight shifting to right leg, club begins to lift off shoulder, muscles tensing.

## Frame 3 - Lift Club
Club raised off shoulder, held in both hands, ogre's arms flexing, preparing for threat display.

## Frame 4 - Raise Club High
Club lifted overhead with both hands, ogre's full height, maximum intimidation pose, muscles bulging.

## Frame 5 - Hold Threat
Maintaining club overhead, roaring pose (mouth wide), peak of intimidation display.

## Frame 6 - Begin Lower
Starting to lower club, mouth closing, muscles still tense but relaxing.

## Frame 7 - Mid Lower
Club lowering back toward shoulder level, arms relaxing, returning to neutral stance.

## Frame 8 - Almost Rest
Club nearly back on shoulder, weight shifting back to center, breathing settling.

## Frame 9 - Complete Loop
Identical to Frame 1, club on shoulder, ready to loop seamlessly.

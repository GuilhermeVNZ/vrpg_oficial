# Goblin 02 - Animation Prompts (9 Frames)

Base prompt: Dungeons and Dragons dark fantasy anime illustration, goblin warrior character variant, green skin with large pointed ears, wearing different armor/weapons than goblin01, standing on circular stone base. Viewed from top-down angle (80-90 degrees), 2D painted illustration style, solid dark grey background NO CHECKERED PATTERN.

---

## Frame 1 - Idle Rest
Goblin at neutral stance, weapons at ready, breathing normally.

## Frame 2 - Begin Movement
Starting to shift, head turning right, weapons adjusting.

## Frame 3 - Mid Turn
Head turned 20 degrees right, body twisting, weapons moving.

## Frame 4 - Peak Turn
Maximum turn right, weapons extended, alert pose.

## Frame 5 - Hold Position
Maintaining turned stance, scanning right side.

## Frame 6 - Begin Return
Starting to turn back center, weapons lowering.

## Frame 7 - Mid Return
Halfway back to neutral, weapons settling.

## Frame 8 - Almost Rest
Nearly neutral, minimal movement.

## Frame 9 - Complete Loop
Identical to Frame 1, ready to loop.
